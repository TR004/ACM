About us

ACM Development LLC. founded in 2002, is a family business specializing in long-term investments in companies and is also a subsidiary of the SOCRATES Private Foundation

Our Services

Value Build-Up

acm functions as management company for the support, administration and further development of investments. We do not only offer capital, but also create significant added value in the realization of development potential for our portfolio companies through our extensive operational and strategic experience.

Investment Goals

At the center of all efforts is the human being - customers, staff and partner companies. In a good and trustful cooperation we always pursue common goals and try to identify and promote the strengths of each individual. We see ourselves as a reliable team player at eye level in every respect and therefore strive for the longest possible partnership. The focus is on medium-sized companies

Real Estate

In the real estate field, we focus on properties with long-term potential for appreciation. The primary goal here is to ensure that our buildings are used in a way that makes sense from an entrepreneurial point of view in the long term.
